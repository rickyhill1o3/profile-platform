Admin Discord Success

Connect your Discord success channel in a few clicks.

Authorize the bot, choose one channel, and every success posted there will be saved to the website and forwarded to the Super Admin success hub.

1. Add the bot to Discord

Click the button below, choose the Discord server, and approve the requested permissions. You do not need to copy IDs, links, or tokens.

Checking connection…

2. Give the bot access to your private success channel

Discord does not automatically add a bot to an existing private channel. Open your success channel in Discord and complete these steps:

  1. Open Edit Channel → Permissions.
  2. Click Add members or roles.
  3. Add the The Shore Shack Success Network bot role.
  4. Allow View Channel, Send Messages, Read Message History, Embed Links, Attach Files, and Manage Messages.

After adding the role, continue to Step 3. Only channels the bot can actually view will appear in the dropdown.

3. Choose your success channel

Select the server and success channel. The list only includes text channels the bot can view. All other Discord channels are ignored.

Super Admin master destination
